The Founder
Sakib
Founder · Golden Hour Production & Hands-Off Growth™

Running my own company was always the dream. Video was always the passion. At 16, I asked my father if I could use his name to open an LLC. Not because I had it all figured out. Because something inside me just knew it was time. He said yes. I never looked back.

I started a video production company — just me, a camera, and an obsession for making things beautiful. By God’s will, I worked with billion-dollar companies. And from day one, I gave 10% of every dollar back. I believe what you give, returns. That principle has never failed me.

“The videos I bled over were sitting in Google Drives. Barely posted. No strategy. These businesses had a weapon and didn’t know how to use it.”

So I sat with my clients and listened. What I heard broke my heart — and lit a fire in me. Brilliant people. Real businesses. No strategy. Overwhelmed. Exhausted.

I realized my job was never just to make beautiful videos. It was to make sure they worked. To be the engine in the background while my clients focused on what only they could do.

Three years of learning, testing, failing, rebuilding. One by one I found my people — researchers, editors, strategists, managers. People who felt the same fire. Today we’re 14. All of us chasing that moment a client says: “I don’t know what you did, but everything changed.”

In January 2026, everything became the H-O-G System. Not just a service — a philosophy. Every client is treated like they’re the only one. Because to us, customer service isn’t a department. It’s who we are.
