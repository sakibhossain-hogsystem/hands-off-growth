The Founder
Sakib
Founder · Golden Hour Production & Hands-Off Growth™

Running his own company was always the dream. Working with video was always the passion. So at 16, he asked his father if he could use his name to open an LLC. Not because he had it all figured out. Because something inside him just knew it was time.

He started a video production company — just him, a camera, and an obsession for making things beautiful. By God’s will, he worked with billion-dollar companies. And from day one, he gave 10% of every dollar back — because he believes what you give, returns. That principle has never failed him.

“The videos I bled over were just sitting there. Collecting dust in a Google Drive. Barely posted. No strategy. These businesses had a weapon and didn’t know how to use it.”

So he sat with his clients and listened. What he heard broke his heart — and lit a fire in him. Brilliant people. Real businesses. Inconsistent teams, no strategy, no idea what converts. Overwhelmed and exhausted.

That’s when he realized — his job was never just to make beautiful videos. It was to make sure they worked. To be the engine in the background while they focused on what only they could do.

He spent the next 3 years learning, testing, failing, and rebuilding. One by one he found his people — researchers, editors, strategists, managers — people who felt that same fire. Today they’re 14, all chasing that moment a client says “I don’t know what you did, but everything changed.”

In January 2026, everything was packaged into the H-O-G System. Not just a service — a philosophy. Every client is treated like they’re the only one. Because to them, customer service isn’t a department. It’s who they are. Welcome to the new era of Golden Hour Production.
